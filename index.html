Green Chili Chicken Enchiladas Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Cooked Chicken Breast
  • Shredded cheddar cheese or Monterey Jack cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Canned diced green chilis
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Cream of Chicken

Directions:

Start by preheating oven to 350 degrees F.As that's preheating you want to mix cream of chicken,sour cream and the chicken breast.
